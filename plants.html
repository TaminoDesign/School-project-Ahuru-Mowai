Experience the fauna of Aotearoa

Wandering the boardwalks will take you by our small lake. You will also come to the Team Tuatara pen where you may spot, if you look carefully, juvenile tuatara basking in the sun. Bird-feeding stations are dotted alongside the boardwalk. Look for kākāriki, tui, korimako (bellbird), hihi (stitchbird), tititi pounamu (rifleman), pīwakawaka (fantail). Don’t forget to check the ground. Takahē are wandering and if you look closely, you may see prod marks in the ground from kiwi beaks.
